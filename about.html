Little ol' Me.

As before, my name is Tariq Moore. I'm a 21 year old college student who currently goes to Broward College Central Campus.
Currently living in Coral Springs of the great state Florida.
I am studying for my Associate of Science in Software Development and slated to graduate in summer of 2022.

Some of my passions include learning about coding and music production!
Learning how to code was not originally what I went to school for. I grew up wanting to create video games and became fascinated with the whole game creation process.
From then onwards I started to learn more about what languages game companies use like C++ and C#. What engines they utilize to create and run my favorite games. Finally, what the development process is like to create said games. Can you tell it's a dream of mine to create one yet?

Music production wise, I can play multiple instruments including Baritone Ukulele, Piano, Alto/Tenor/Baritone Saxophone, and soon Bass Guitar. I can't play everything so I use FL Studio to play virtual instruments or VSTs we call it. Hit play above to listen to something I made!
