Portfolio Research

Best way to start this is with the classic question, What is a portfolio website?

At its most basic form, a portfolio website provides professional information about an individual or a company and presents a showcase of their work. - Trevin Shirey (#4)

The intent of a portfolio is to give site visitors an idea about what we the portfolio owner can do in a show, don't tell style.
Employers want to see what we can do and if we understand what we write.

My portfolio should showcase to the visitor what skill sets I have attained through school or personal projects at home.
It should give an idea of my character, values, and my drive for learning about my choice of career being front-end development.

But most of all.

I hope my portfolio shows my artistic passion as I feel programming is an art.
